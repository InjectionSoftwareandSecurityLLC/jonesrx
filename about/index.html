Deeply rooted in the ATL and Columbus, GA music scenes with connections in Austin, TX, Jones RX thrives on an independent DIY spirit, releasing music through Swelter Sounds.

Jones RX made their debut in 2023 with the Starstruck EP, marking an entrance into the alternative and hyper pop scene that's hard to ignore. Their latest album, Lost CTRL, has already gained a dedicated following, with standout tracks like New Low (feat. SteelPraud) and Upside Down.

With tracks produced by the likes of RJ Pasin, Cian Treacy, and other prominent musicians in the hyperpop scene, Jones crafted Lost CTRL to blend personal insight with high-energy, genre-bending sound.

A fan of whiskey, huskies, and all things cyberpunk — Jones channels a unique blend of futuristic and nostalgic elements into their artistry. Jones RX is a fresh force in the music world, and this is only the beginning.
